Burj Khalifa

About the Monument

Burj Khalifa is a skyscraper in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. Standing at 828 meters (2,717 ft), it's the world's tallest building since its completion in 2009. The tower features Islamic architecture patterns and houses offices, residences, and the Armani Hotel.
