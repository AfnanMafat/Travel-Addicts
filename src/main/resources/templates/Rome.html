About the City

Rome, Italy's capital, is a sprawling metropolis with nearly 3,000 years of globally influential art, architecture, and culture. From the Colosseum to Vatican City, Rome's historic center is a UNESCO World Heritage Site preserving ancient ruins and Renaissance masterpieces.
