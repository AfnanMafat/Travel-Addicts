The Statue of Unity

World's Tallest Statue

About the Monument

Standing tall at 182 meters (597 feet), the Statue of Unity is a tribute to Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, India's first Deputy Prime Minister and the 'Iron Man of India'. Located in Gujarat, this engineering marvel was inaugurated on October 31, 2018.
