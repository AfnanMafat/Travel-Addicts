About the Monument

The Statue of Liberty is a colossal neoclassical sculpture on Liberty Island in New York Harbor. Gifted by France in 1886, this copper statue represents Libertas, the Roman goddess of freedom, holding a torch and a tabula ansata inscribed with July 4, 1776.
